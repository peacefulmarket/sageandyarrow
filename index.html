Hello, my name is Sarah.
I'm originally from NYC on the Westside of Midtown known as "Hell's Kitchen." Today, I live in Boise, Idaho with my fabulous partner, Kelley, my two clever sons (ages 8 and 11), and one strikingly handsome cat named Kittay. Although Boise is a place that's somewhat off-the-radar for many big city folks, I find that it's a vibrant little city surrounded by natural beauty and a great place to call home.

On this website I have included some pictures from a recent visit to "Freak Alley," an outdoor gallery which was created by local artists in the heart of downtown off 8th Street and Idaho.

I have also included some images to download from the Public Domain which are free for commercial or personal use. There are quotes, poems, info about my favorite music artists links, and more.
